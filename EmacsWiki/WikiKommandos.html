Wiki Kommandos

Here is a summary of keystrokes available in every Wiki buffer:

C-c C-a jump to an index of all the Wiki pages C-c C-b show all pages that reference this page C-c C-s search for a word in your Wiki pages C-c C-f jump to another Wiki page; prompts for the name C-c C-l highlight/refresh the current buffer C-c C-p publish any Wiki pages that have changed as HTML C-c C-r rename wiki link at point C-c C-v change wiki project C-c C-D delete wiki link at point (binding will only work on X) C-c = diff this page against the last backup version TAB move to the next Wiki reference S-TAB move to the previous Wiki reference

Die Sourcen befinden sich unter: ~/elisp/emacs-wiki.el
